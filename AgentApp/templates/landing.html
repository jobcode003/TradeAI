About TradeAI

The Origin

Founded in 2025 by an elite computer science specialist in machine learning and AI, and a seasoned professional trader, TradeAI was developed to remove emotional bias from trading. It empowers traders to make data-driven decisions with confidence, eliminating fear and speculation from every trade.

The Mission

Our mission is to democratize institutional-grade market intelligence. We believe every trader—regardless of experience or background—should have access to the same powerful tools used by hedge funds, making trading simple, accessible, and effective for everyone.

The Technology

Powered by advanced Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) models trained on clean, factual, and historical market data, our trading agent analyzes thousands of data points instantly to deliver optimal trading signals. With over 18 specialized models covering major forex pairs across multiple timeframes, TradeAI provides precise, data-driven insights for confident trading decisions.
